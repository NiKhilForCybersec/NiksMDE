Incident Management Best Practices
  • Work incidents, not just alerts: Incidents provide context; avoid cherry-picking alerts
  • Use Attack Story first: Get the big picture before diving into details
  • Document as you go: Add comments during investigation, not just at the end
  • Classify accurately: Good classification improves future detection
  • Check related incidents: Same user/device may have connected activity
  • Review AIR findings: Automated investigation often finds additional evidence

Daily Incident Operations

  • Review high-severity incidents first thing each shift
  • Check for incidents pending action approval
  • Investigate assigned incidents to resolution
  • Escalate complex incidents to senior analysts
  • Document findings and close resolved incidents
  • Update shift handoff notes with active incidents
