Best Practices
  • Data retention: Set to 180 days for full investigation capability
  • Enable key features: AIR, Live Response, Tamper Protection, EDR block mode
  • RBAC: Follow least privilege - operators for SOC, readers for stakeholders
  • Device groups: Organize by environment, business unit, or sensitivity
