Prioritization Tips
  • Critical/High first: Always address these immediately
  • Check for patterns: Multiple related alerts may indicate campaign
  • Use MITRE mapping: Understand attack stage
  • Correlate with incidents: Alerts may be part of larger incident
  • Classify all alerts: Improves ML and helps metrics
